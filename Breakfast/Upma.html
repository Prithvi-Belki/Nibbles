Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Water
  • Mustard seeds
  • Chia Seeds
  • Peanuts
  • Coriander
  • Curry LEaves
  • Onions
  • Chilli
  • Salt
  • Lemon Juice
  • Sugar
  • Sooji

Description

Upma is a flavorful, tasty, savory and popular South Indian breakfast that is made with cream of wheat or semolina flour (called rava or suji), a few lentils, nuts, vegetables, herbs and spices.

Steps

  1. Dry roast 1/2 cup sooji, and keep it aside.
  2. In a pan heat oil, add Peanuts, Onion, Cumin seeds, Mustard seeds, green Chilli in that order along with the curry leaves.
  3. Add water, Salt, Sugar, Lemon Juice, and mix it thoroughly.
  4. Add roasted Rava, and keep stirring to avoid lumps, keep cocking until water is completely absorbed.
  5. Once it is cooked, mix it well, and your Upma is ready to eat.
