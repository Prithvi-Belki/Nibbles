Pepper-Chicken

Ingredients

  • Pepper
  • Chilli
  • Coriander
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Ginger-Garlic
  • Oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Curry Leaves

Description

Pepper chicken is a popular South Indian chicken preparation.

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a kadai. Add in fennel seeds and let them sizzle. After that add Onion, Curry Leaves, and Green Chilli. And Saute till golden Brown.
  2. Add Ginger Garlic paste, and Saute for 2 Minutes.
  3. Add in Spice powder and Salt.
  4. Saute it for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add the Chicken that was washed.
  6. Mix well till the masala coats the chicken
  7. Cover the Lid and cook it for 20 minutes. Later cook the chicken in High Flame for another 3-4 Minutes.
  8. Add the coarsely ground black pepper and mix well. Add Lemon Juice & Coriander Leaves and Mix well.
  9. Your Pepper chicken is ready for the serving.
