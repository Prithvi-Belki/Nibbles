Poha

Description

Poha is a most popular breakfast or snack recipe made with flattened rice(poha), onions, roasted peanuts, and a few spices

Ingredients

  • Poha
  • Chilli
  • Coriander
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Mustard
  • Oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Sugar
  • Curry Leaves

Steps

  1. Rinse 2-3 cups of poha in water and let it soak for a minute and drain the water out using a strainer.
  2. Sprinkle Â½ teaspoon salt, sugar, coriander, Lemon juice and mix gently, after the water is drained.
  3. In a pan heat oil, add Peanuts, Onion, Cumin seeds, Mustard seeds, green Chilli in that order along with the curry leaves.
  4. Saute it for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add half a teaspoon of turmeric powder, under the medium flame.
  6. Lastly, add the poha that is already mixed with salt, Sugar, Coriander, Lemon juice and mix well.
  7. Give it a quick taste and adjust salt if needed.
  8. Easy, delicious and nutritious Poha is ready!!
